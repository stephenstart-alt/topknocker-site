FAQ

Do I have to go door knocking?
Not at all. Many agents use TopKnocker purely for targeted letters and mailshots ‚Äî it's often more effective than knocking. The intelligence works for any outreach method: letters, calls, emails, or visits. You pick the approach that suits your style.
What is the Motivation Score?
Every property gets a score from 0‚Äì100 based on six weighted signals: ownership tenure, EPC activity, market status (withdrawn, reduced, SSTC fallen through), planning applications, and whether multiple signals overlap. A score of 70+ means multiple indicators suggest this owner is likely to sell soon.
How do the letters work?
Click "Send Letter" on any property and the system generates a personalised letter using real comparable sales data from their area. It auto-selects the right template based on why the property scored high ‚Äî a different tone for a long-term owner vs. someone whose sale fell through. How does the weekly market scanner work?
Every Monday at 7am, TopKnocker automatically scans every listing across your territory. It compares this week's listings against last week's and detects new listings, price reductions, withdrawals, and fallen-through sales. It even checks Rightmove to find out if a disappeared listing was sold STC, under offer, or genuinely withdrawn. All changes are logged to your dashboard automatically.
What is the Market Changes dashboard?
It's a live feed showing every listing change detected by the scanner ‚Äî new properties coming to market, price reductions, withdrawals, and sales. You can filter by change type, search by street or postcode, and see which agent was involved. It's designed to give your team a quick Monday morning briefing on what happened across your patch.
How is this different from Rightmove or OnTheMarket data?
Portals show what's already listed ‚Äî by then it's too late. TopKnocker shows you who's about to list, using ownership tenure, EPC activity, planning applications, and failed sale signals. You're reaching out before anyone else knows they're thinking of selling.
