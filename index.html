How it works

A simple workflow your team can run every week.

Step 1

Activate your territory

Tell us your core patch (towns/areas). We set up a high-quality target list for your team.

Step 2

Filter for “hot” owners

Find owners held 10+ years, warm pockets, and signals that suggest movement.

Step 3

Time your outreach

Use on-market status signals (where available) to time knocks and letters — new listings, reductions, and SSTC.

Step 4

Track performance

Log outcomes, run a leaderboard, and turn canvassing into a measurable system.

What you get

Everything focused on one outcome: more valuations from fewer doors.

FAQ

Is this only for door knocking?
It’s designed for canvassing, but many agencies use the same target list for letters, mailshots, and calling.
How do territories work?
You tell us your patch (county / towns / areas). We activate a tailored territory pack so your team has a strong, usable list.
How quickly can we start?
After the demo, we can typically activate your territory and onboard your team quickly. (Coventry/CV excluded.)
Do you offer exclusivity?
Optional on Regional plans depending on territory. We’ll discuss this on the demo.
What data sources do you use?
Ownership and sale history are derived from official datasets (e.g. HM Land Registry Price Paid). Where available, we also surface on-market listing signals (e.g., listed / reduced / Sold STC) to help you time outreach.
